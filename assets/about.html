About us

Ateliers Z+A, a designer firm based in Geneva, was established in 2000 by Thoby ZA after his long term experience with designer firms in Swiss, such as Herzog & de Meuron and Graber Pulver Architekten. Many of his clients, like Gramazio & Kohler followed him in his new adventures.

fter a fortunate encounter with Swiss Architecture Organizations in 2010, Ateliers Z+A recruited more fundamental members as our creative and designer team. By 2015 our firm was already collaborating with big international brands such as Barilla, Volkswagen, Campari, Vodafone, Philips, Kraft, and Fila. In 2018 and 2019 our firm awarded as the Greenest designer firm in Swiss by the Swiss Architecture Organizations.
